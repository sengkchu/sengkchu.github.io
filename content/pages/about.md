My name is Seng Kin Chu, and I am the Coding Disciple. I am 100% self-taught in Python, and I am a data science enthusiast.
I am passionate about learning and I strive to improve myself every single day. 

I used to work as a traditional chemical process engineer. My job was to act as an overseer for a well established pharmaceutical production process.
It was a slow and steady job that provided me with a decent income, but I was not happy. Eventually, I became very complacent with my job and reached a point of stagnation.
I wanted more, I wanted fulfillment in life. I didn't want to settle into a job for money alone. I knew I needed to change, so I quit my job.
At the time, I had no idea what I was doing in my life. After 11 months of wandering, I discovered Python and data science. I immediately fell in love with coding.

I created this blog to document my journey of personal development. I also hope to inspire other people with the content on this blog.

Hope you enjoy your stay!
